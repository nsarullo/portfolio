Developed two full Entity Relationship Diagrams for "The Tennis Shack". These diagrams included all entities, attributes, primary key specifications, minimum and maximum cardinalities, and entity relationships.

The first diagram displayed relationships with attributes, non-binary relationships, and M:N (many to many) relationships.
In the second diagram all of these relationships were resolved into associative entities.
